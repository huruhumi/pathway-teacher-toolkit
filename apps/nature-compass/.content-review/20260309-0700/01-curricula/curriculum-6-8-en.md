# Curriculum: 6-8 (EN)

Theme: 城市湿地生态探索
City: 武汉
Duration: 180min
Lessons: 4

## Lesson 1: Water Wonders and Floating Plants
- **Theme:** Wetland Plant Life
- **Location:** East Lake (Donghu) Greenway - Wetland Section
- **STEAM Focus:** Science, Art
- **Goals:** Identify and name 3 common wetland plants in English.; Observe and record plant buoyancy in a field journal.; Practice using the sentence structure 'I see a [color] [plant].'
- **Activities:** Students will go on a 'Plant Scavenger Hunt' along the East Lake shore to find floating and rooted plants. They will conduct a simple buoyancy experiment by placing different natural items in the water to see what floats.
- **Safety:** Keep a 2-meter distance from the water's edge; wear life vests if approaching docks.

## Lesson 2: Feathered Friends of Wuhan
- **Theme:** Bird Observation and Habitats
- **Location:** Jiefang Park Wetland Garden
- **STEAM Focus:** Science, Technology, Art
- **Goals:** Use binoculars to spot and count 3 different bird species.; Understand why birds need wetlands for food and shelter.; Use basic adjectives to describe bird movements (fast, slow, high, low).
- **Activities:** Students will use binoculars to observe migratory birds and map out where they find 'homes' in the park. They will then sketch a bird nest using twigs and dried grass found on the ground.
- **Safety:** Avoid feeding the birds; stay on the designated wooden boardwalks to protect habitats.

## Lesson 3: The Tiny World of Insects
- **Theme:** Insects and Life Cycles
- **Location:** Sha Lake (Shahu) Park
- **STEAM Focus:** Science, Engineering
- **Goals:** Discover and identify 3 types of wetland insects (e.g., dragonfly).; Compare the number of legs and wings on different bugs.; Learn the 'Head, Thorax, Abdomen' song in English.
- **Activities:** Using magnifying glasses, students will explore the tall grass areas to find insects. They will act out the life cycle of a dragonfly from water-dweller to flyer through a movement-based game.
- **Safety:** Wear long pants to prevent bug bites; do not touch unknown insects; apply sunscreen.

## Lesson 4: Muddy Engineers: Wetland Soil
- **Theme:** Soil Filtration and Building
- **Location:** Hankou Jiangtan Wetland Park
- **STEAM Focus:** Engineering, Mathematics
- **Goals:** Differentiate between dry sand and wet mud by touch and sight.; Build a simple 'mini-dam' to see how wetlands slow down water.; Use the verbs 'build', 'pour', and 'stop' in context.
- **Activities:** Students will participate in a 'Mud Kitchen' where they test how water moves through different soil types. They will work in teams to build a small barrier using rocks and mud to protect a toy animal's home.
- **Safety:** Ensure all students wash hands thoroughly after touching soil; stay within the marked 'Discovery Zone'.
